FAQ's

Q: Can I request a special serenade from a singing tulip?

A: As enchanting as that may sound, our flowers specialize in visual beauty rather than vocal talents. However, we're confident that our blossoms will make your heart sing with delight!

Q: Do you offer a "Forget-Me-Not" guarantee?

A: While we can't control memory lapses, we guarantee the quality and freshness of our flowers. If, for any reason, you're not satisfied with your purchase, please reach out to our team, and we'll do our best to make things right.

Q: Can I order a bouquet for my pet rock?

A: Absolutely! Flowers have a way of brightening any space, even if it's a cozy corner occupied by a pet rock. Treat your rock to a burst of natural colors and watch it embrace the floral magic!
